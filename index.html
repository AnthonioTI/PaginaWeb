Welcome to CBC

We are honored that you have taken the time to visit our website. Thank you!

 

At Crossroads Bible Church, our very name is indicative of who we are. We live at the crossroads of the world. We are a local gathering of God's people. And the Bible, our middle name, is the basis of everything we do.

 

Crossroads offers you the opportunity to build your relationship with God and other people in a bilingual, interdenominational, multicultural setting. If you don't already belong to a local church family, we invite you to consider becoming a part of the CBC family.

 

We have three identical services every Sunday. The first service starts at 8:30 AM and ends at 9:45 AM.  The second service starts at 11:15 AM and ends at 12:30 PM.  The third service starts at 6:00 PM and ends at 7:15 PM.   A professional nursery is available during all three services for children up through 3 years of age, and Sunday School classes are available for children, youth and adults of all ages from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM.

 

We hope you enjoy looking around our website. If there's anything we can do for you, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. We're here to serve you.

 

Blessings!

 

Pastor Steve

How to Become a Biblical Christian

Receiving Jesus Christ as your personal Savior and Lord is the most important decision that you could ever make. If you have never made the decision to put your trust in the Lord Jesus and to allow Him to take control of you life, we urge you to do so.

 

The booklet entitled “Steps to Peace with God”, available at the CBC Sanctuary, explains clearly what the Bible teaches about how you can enter into a personal relationship with your Creator and receive His free gift of eternal life. Please take time to read it and to apply what it says to your life.

 

At Crossroads, we recognize that receiving Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord is a personal decision of faith between you and God. We respect the very personal nature of this significant decision, but also want you to know that we would love to share the joy of praying with you and assisting you in this eternally important matter if you would like for us to do so. If you decide to make this decision, or if you would like to know more about what it all means, please don’t hesitate to contact us. We would count it a great privilege to help you.

How to Become a Member of CBC

All who confess the Lord Jesus Christ as their personal Savior, who desire to live in obedience to Christ, and who subscribe to the Crossroads Bible Church (CBC) statement of faith are eligible for membership in this body.

 

Persons interested in membership should indicate their desire by speaking with one of the elders, communicating with the church office, or by putting a note in one of the offering boxes.

 

Those individuals who express interest in becoming members of CBC will be asked to complete a short application form and to attend one-session seminar where basic information regarding the church is explained.

 

At this seminar each person is also given the opportunity to share a brief personal testimony and to affirm his/her agreement with CBC doctrinal statement. Once accepted, new members are formally welcomed into the church during a Sunday Worship Service.
